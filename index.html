About Me

A little background on my journey to where I am now. I completed my Accounting degree in 2008 and worked in the audit industry for a couple of years, gaining insight into the financial aspects of companies. I then moved over into private industry where I have been working up and till the present.

With the Covid lockdowns in 2020 and working from home, I had enough time to sit and reflect on where I would like to take the next part of my life, career and my journey as well as leveraging my experience I have gained over the preceding years and combining that with my interests.

So I started on a new journey into data and how it can be used to enhance our insights and drive our decisions that we make. Helping to improve those decisions and bettering ourselves in almost spheres.

Apart from data and the assortment of technologies that go with that, I also have a keen interest in history, politics, languages, Sci-Fi and fantasy, fiction and non-fiction. I love reading biographies as it teaches you so much of how someone dealt with situations and how I can draw inspiration from that. The most profound biography that I have read has been Barack Obama's - A promised land.
